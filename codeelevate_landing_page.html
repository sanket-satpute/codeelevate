Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Click on any question to learn more.

Can I join if I'm in college?

✕

Absolutely! We welcome students from all backgrounds. Whether you're in your first year or final year, our course is designed to complement your academic journey and give you practical coding skills that will boost your career prospects.

Is this only for freshers?

✕

Not at all! Anyone can join regardless of background. We've had successful students from various fields - engineers, arts graduates, working professionals, and even those looking for a career change. Our curriculum starts from the very basics.

What's the price after demo?

✕

Great question! We'll discuss pricing details during the demo class. We believe in transparency and want you to experience our teaching quality first. Our fees are competitive and we offer flexible payment options to make learning accessible.

Do I need any prior programming knowledge?

✕

Zero experience required! We start from the absolute basics - what is programming, how computers work, and then gradually build up to advanced Java concepts. All you need is curiosity and dedication to learn.

What if I miss some classes?

✕

No worries! We provide recorded sessions for all classes, plus our mentors offer one-on-one catch-up sessions. We also have a supportive peer community where you can get help and stay on track with your learning.

Will I get job assistance after the course?

✕

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive job assistance including resume building, interview preparation, mock interviews, and connecting you with our network of hiring partners. Your success is our success!
